    2-in-1 versatility, one incredible touchscreen

    • Lightweight starting at 879 g (1.94 lbs).1 Carry the tablet that can replace your laptop
    • Virtually edge-to-edge 13” PixelSense™ touchscreen designed for pen2 and Windows 11
    • Sign, sketch or navigate with Surface Slim Pen 2, securely stored and charging wirelessly in Surface Pro Signature Keyboard2

    Battery that keeps going and going

    • Surface Pro 9 with IntelFootnote® 12th Gen has up to 15.5 hours of battery life3
    • Surface Pro 9 with 5G4 with Microsoft SQFootnote® 3 has up to 19 hours of battery life3
    • Extra battery life means fewer stops to charge along the way

    The most powerful Pro does it all

    • IntelFootnote® Evo™ 5 12th Gen processor, delivers blazing fast performance
    • Perfect for full desktop productivity and local gaming
    • 2 x USB-CFootnote® ports let you easily use or charge accessories, dock to high-powered displays, or transfer large creative files with ease. Choose Surface Pro 9 with IntelFootnote® for ultra-fast Thunderbolt™ 4 ports

    Surface Pro 9 (5G) keeps you connected

    • Stay connected anywhere. Surface Pro 9 (5G) with Microsoft SQFootnote® 3 keeps you connected with high-speed 5G4
    • Unlock Neural Processing Unit (NPU) powered camera experiences including Automatic Framing and Eye Contact
